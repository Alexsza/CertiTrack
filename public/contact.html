>Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our CertiTrack digital diploma and validation app.

A blockchain digital diploma is a secure URL link giving access to a tamper-proof and directly verifiable certificate. Students can use this link, and employers or third parties can verify the certificate's authenticity with a single click, without technical skills.

CertiTrack's digital diploma system allows the digitization of academic and professional qualifications, diplomas, and certifications. It also supports micro-certification to certify specific skills, enhancing the value of longer courses or degrees.

CertiTrack provides NCI with a platform to digitize diplomas and certifications. The platform enables easy issuance of secure "blockchain digital credentials" that can be shared and accessed via URL links. No technical skills are required to use them.
