Welcome to CertiTrack, where education validation meets innovation!
In collaboration with NCI, we're transforming the way academic achievements are recognized. Our platform empowers students, institutions, and employers to seamlessly authenticate digital diplomas through secure blockchain technology.

To embark on this journey, make sure you have:


  • Crypto Wallet with Metamask to manage your NFT securely
  • Passcode sent to your Student Email (valid for 24 hours only)
  • NCI Diploma or Certificate from 2022 or later
